Shrimp Spaghetti

Description

A delicious spaghetti dish with large shrimp in a tomato sauce.

This dish will surely liven up spaghetti meals with the main focus not being the meat but seafood.

Ingredients

  • 20 large shrimp, cut head and tail off
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 bunch flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 pounds spaghetti

Steps

  1. Fill pan with water and bring to a boil. Put shrimps in the pan then reduce heat to low and let simmer, about 45 minutes.
  2. Combines tomatoes and parsley in a blender; puree until smooth.
  3. Cook spaghetti.
