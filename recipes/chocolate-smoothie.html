Shrimp Spaghetti

Description

A delicious chocolate smoothie to cool you off on a hot day.

This smoothie is full of nutrients and is very yummy.

Ingredients

  • 2 very ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 1.5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine bananas, milk, ice cubes, cocoa powder, peanut butter, and vanilla ina blender; blend on high speed until smooth. Pour into glasses.
